# Sarajah Store Annual Report Dashboard (MIS Project)

## Project Overview
The **Sarajah Store Annual Report Dashboard** is an interactive Management Information System (MIS) dashboard developed in **Microsoft Excel**. It transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and Excel visualization tools. The dashboard enables users to monitor business performance and make informed decisions.

---

## Project Objectives
- Analyze annual sales performance.
- Identify top-performing customer segments.
- Compare sales across different sales channels.
- Monitor order status and operational efficiency.
- Evaluate state-wise sales performance.
- Track monthly sales trends for better forecasting.

---

## Tools & Technologies
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts

---

## Dashboard Features
- Sales Distribution (Men vs Women)
- Sales by Order Channel
- Order Status Analysis
- Age Group vs Gender Analysis
- Monthly Sales & Orders Trend
- Monthly Sales Index by Age Group
- Top 5 Performing States
- Interactive Filters (Month, Channel, Category)

---

## Key Business Insights
- Women customers contribute **64%** of total sales.
- Amazon is the leading sales channel with **35%** of total orders.
- **92%** of orders are successfully delivered, indicating strong operational efficiency.
- The **Adult** age group generates the highest sales.
- **Maharashtra** is the top-performing state by revenue.
- Monthly trends help identify seasonal demand patterns.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on women and adult customers.
- Strengthen sales in high-performing states while improving low-performing regions.
- Increase investment in successful sales channels like Amazon.
- Improve underperforming channels through promotional strategies.
- Reduce cancellations and returns to enhance customer satisfaction.
